GUIDELINES

HOW TO CREATE SIMPLE TEXT

Simple text is a text that is rewritten from its original version to a more comprehensible rendition.

By for example removing ambiguous meanings and words, rewriting and making sentences shorter the text can be made easier to understand.

Persons that are in need of simple text may have different preferences when it comes to simple text. This is because the difficulties vary from person to person.

Here are some guidelines that will help you when creating a simple text. Remember that these tips shouldn't be followed to strictly, use common sense and make changes as you see fit.

  • Change complex words to more common synonyms.
  • Change words with more than 6 letters to shorter synonyms if possible.
  • Write simple and unambiguous sentences.
  • Try to make the context as clear and simple as possible.
  • Create a simple layout.
